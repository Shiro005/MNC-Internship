Expanding the existing business with the formation of a professional board

The company sought to secure its future by involving the next generation. Encountered obstacles due to the lack of clarity regarding roles and responsibilities

Industry :

Metal

Business Line:

Manufacturing

Area of work
  • Facilitating the establishment of a professional board to address duties and responsibilities Developing a clear and effective hierarchical structure and remuneration framework
    • Developed Professional Board

    Collaborated closely with the owners and the next generation to develop the professional board. This involved a comprehensive assessment of the company's organizational structure, current and future business needs, and the skills and capabilities of the individuals entering the business.

    Defined Roles

    Assessing the capabilities of the next generation and aligning them with the company's needs led to enhanced performance and productivity Ensured that each family member entering the business had a defined role that matched their skill set. This approach helped mitigate the risk of dissatisfaction, underperformance, or conflicts within the organization.

    Impact

    Professional Stand in the Market The manufacturing company underwent a significant transformation. Improved structure and streamlined processes increased operational efficiency, effective decision-making, and enhanced customer trust and satisfaction.
